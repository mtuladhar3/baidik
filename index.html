✦ Who We Are

Baidik Investment Company Limited

Baidik Investment Company Limited is a specialized investment company headquartered in Handigaun, Kathmandu, Nepal, dedicated to the hydropower energy sector. As of July 16, 2025 (Ashad 32, 2082), the company maintains a robust paid-up capital of NPR 82.95 Crore. The company is strategically positioned to capitalize on Nepal's ambitious national targets for hydropower expansion. It is a well-capitalized, focused investment vehicle operating in Nepal's high-growth hydropower sector. With a clear mandate to invest heavily in the sector, Baidik Investment Company Limited aims to generate substantial profits from both listed and unlisted renewable energy assets. The company's clear strategic mandate, and diversified (though concentrated) portfolio of listed and unlisted hydropower assets position it for potential value creation as Nepal pursues ambitious renewable energy targets.

✦ Corporate Overview

Baidik Investment Company Limited, has its registered office at BG Group Pvt. Ltd., Kotal Marg, Hadigaun, Kathmandu, Nepal. The company is governed by two Directors, namely, Mrs. Un Maharjan and Mrs. Kabita Lamichhane.

Baidik Investment Company Limited is exclusively focused on investing in Nepal's hydropower projects to generate renewable energy. The company serves as a dedicated investment vehicle channeling domestic capital into the rapidly expanding hydroelectricity sector, which is recognized by the Government of Nepal as a backbone for future economic prosperity.

Strategic Focus and Business Plan

Baidik Investment Company Limited has articulated a clear and ambitious strategic plan to significantly increase its capital allocation toward Nepal's hydropower sector.

Market Growth Drivers

Nepal possesses an estimated 83,000 MW of hydropower potential, of which approximately 43,000 MW is considered economically viable. Currently, less than 10% of this potential has been developed, presenting substantial growth opportunities.

Investment Strategy

Listed Securities (Fair Value Through Profit or Loss, FVTPL): Investments in publicly traded hydropower companies providing liquidity and mark-to-market gains. Unlisted Securities (Fair Value Through Profit or Loss, FVTPL): Strategic investments in developmental-stage hydropower projects offering higher growth potential and entry at lower valuations.
