Shiva Shree Hydropower Pvt. Ltd

Shivashree Hydropower Limited is a limited company registered and incorporated in Nepal under the company act 2063 having registered office at Buddhanagar, Kathmandu. The sole purpose of the company is serving the nation with clean energy by developing and operating hydropower projects in Nepal. The corporate office of the company is located at Buddhanagar, Kathmandu. The shares of this company is listed on NEPSE as SSHL on 6th Kartik 2077

The company received Generation license for Upper Chaku ‘A’ Hydroelectric Project for construction of the project with installed capacity 22.2 MW and concluded the Power Purchase Agreement with Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) on 22nd Bhadra, 2067.

Khani Khola Hydropower Co. Ltd

Khani Khola Hydropower Was Established on BS 2069-06-17 under the Company act 2063. The Company was converted to Public Limited Company on BS 2069-12-14. The basic objective of the company to generate electricity. The Company is Located at 187 Shree Gorakhnath Marga, Dhumbaraki, Kathmandu -4.

The Company has developed developed Tungun – Thosne Hydropower (4.36 MW) and Khani Khola Hydropower (2 MW) at Sankhau, Bhattedanda and Ikudol of South Lalitpur.

Singati Hydro Energy Limited

Singati Hydro Energy Ltd. Singati Hydro Energy Ltd. was established to spearhead the development of the Singati Khola Hydropower Project (25MW). Nestled in the northeastern part of Dolakha district, this project stands out as one of the Super Six initiatives awarded to the private sector through a rigorous national bidding process. Setting itself apart, Singati Hydro Energy Ltd. undertook the monumental task of excavating a 6-kilometer-long tunnel and laying a 26-kilometer-long Transmission Line, marking significant milestones in the project's execution. Total paid up capital of the company is worth NPR 2.9 Billion.

Habitat Power Company Limited

Habitat Power Company is a testament to a bold vision, fueled by a commitment to lead the charge in developing and constructing transformative hydropower and transmission line projects. It’s mission is to radiate sustainable electricity across the diverse landscapes of Nepal.
